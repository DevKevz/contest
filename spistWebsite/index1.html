ACCESS DEPARTMENT

The ACCESS Department is a combination of computer experts and specialized students from the fields of Information Technology (BSIT), Computer Science (BSCS), and Computer Engineering (BSCpE).

Who We Are

ACCESS is dedicated to bridging the gap between theory and practice, ensuring that students gain not only a strong academic foundation but also hands-on experience with real-world applications. By integrating cutting-edge technologies, industry-driven projects, and collaborative learning ACCESS equips students with the technical expertise, problem-solving abilities, and innovative mindset required to thrive in today’s rapidly evolving tech industry. Through workshops, research initiatives, hackathons, internships, and partnerships with industry leaders, ACCESS provides students with practical exposure to emerging trends such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and software development. By fostering a culture of continuous learning and adaptability, ACCESS prepares its members to take on the challenges of the digital age, ensuring they remain competitive and ready to contribute to the advancement of technology.

OUR EXPERTISE

BSIT

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program focuses on...

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program focuses on providing students with a comprehensive understanding of IT solutions, cybersecurity, and web development. It is designed to equip future professionals with the technical skills and problem-solving abilities needed to address modern technological challenges. In the field of IT solutions, BSIT students learn how to design, develop, and implement technology-driven systems that improve efficiency and productivity across various industries. This includes software applications, enterprise systems, cloud computing, and database management, all of which play a crucial role in modern business operations.

BSCS

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) program is centered on...

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) program is centered on the study of algorithms, artificial intelligence (AI), and software development, providing students with a deep understanding of computational theories, problem-solving techniques, and advanced programming concepts. This program prepares students to design and develop innovative software solutions that drive technological advancements across various industries. In the field of algorithms, BSCS students learn to analyze, design, and optimize computational processes that enable efficient problem-solving. They explore data structures, complexity analysis, and algorithmic paradigms such as divide and conquer, dynamic programming, and graph theory. These fundamental concepts are essential for developing high-performance applications and systems.

BSCpE

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) program is designed to equip students...

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) program is designed to equip students with expertise in hardware engineering, embedded systems, and robotics. This interdisciplinary field merges the principles of electrical engineering and computer science, enabling graduates to design, develop, and optimize computing hardware and intelligent systems. In the field of hardware engineering, BSCpE students study the architecture and design of computer components, microprocessors, circuit boards, and networking devices. They gain hands-on experience in digital logic design, computer architecture, and system integration, allowing them to develop and enhance computing hardware for various applications, from consumer electronics to high-performance computing.
